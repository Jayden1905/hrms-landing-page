Office HR HRMS Features

Office HR provides an all-in-one human resource solution for efficient HR operations.

download app

Attendance

Make an easy check-in/out with Office HR HRMS App. Auto, QR scan, Office or Remote check-in/out will make your company more structured & save your time.

Leave

Check your leave availability & request a leave with your mobile phone. No need to fill a form to request leave if you have Office HR HRMS App. Instant leave request & approval.

Department Directory

Arrange your organization's internal structure more adequately with Office HR HRMS. Office HR Department Directory will show to employees who is in-charge to whom.

Announcement

Are you still sending emails to your employees to inform them of something? The Office HRMS software will help you track & monitor whether or not all employees received the announcement.

Payslip

Collect your monthly payslip in your mobiles. Office HR will enhance the transparency between employer & employee. Every employee can view the detailed payslip with all additions & deductions with Office HR App.
